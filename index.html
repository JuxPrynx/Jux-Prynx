About Jux Prynx

Jux Prynx, (Prince Obeng Asamoah) is a US-based Ghanaian Worship Leader, Recording Artiste, Music Director, and Songwriter, dedicated to the ministry of spreading the gospel of Christ through music. Jux Prynx started his music career in his church as a worship leader, trumpeter, and keyboardist. He performed with choirs at various levels and formed a group called The Higher Praise Choir. He began organizing his gospel concert in 2016, dubbed YI NAYE (Praise Him), which featured sensational Ghanaian and Nigerian musicians. He has also been recognized for his exceptional talent and dedication to his ministry such as:

  • Ghana Gospel Music Awards 2022 (Best Video of the Year: Hosanna, Urban Gospel Artist of the Year)
  • Youth Eminence Awards 2021(Male Vocalist: Hosanna, New artist of the year and Afro-Pop: Hosanna)
  • EM Awards 2022(Male Vocalist of the Year: Life, Music Video of the year: Hosanna)

Jux plans to win more souls through his ministry and strives for the best.
